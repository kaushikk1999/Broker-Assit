# Phase 1 — User Personas

_Buyer-first (B2B). Primary personas decide/buy; secondary personas use the embedded widget and shape
what the demos must prove._

## PRIMARY — Brokerage buyers (decision-makers)

### P1. Product / Growth Lead — "Ananya" (mid-size brokerage)
- **Goal:** Differentiate support UX, deflect tickets, improve activation — without a long build.
- **Needs:** Fast embed, multilingual reach into Tier-2/3 investors, measurable deflection, demo proof.
- **Frustrations:** App-only bots take months; vendors over-promise; no Hindi/Tamil finance options.
- **Decision criteria:** Time-to-embed, language coverage, citation/trust story, integration effort.
- **What the site must show her:** live demo working in EN/HI/TA with citations; "one snippet" embed.

### P2. Compliance / Risk Officer — "Rahul"
- **Goal:** Ensure assistant answers are sourced, disclaimered, and SEBI/NSE-safe.
- **Needs:** Citations on every answer, controllable knowledge base, audit trail, clear disclaimers.
- **Frustrations:** AI that "makes things up"; no source traceability; algo content compliance risk.
- **Decision criteria:** Citation rigor, content governance, disclaimer handling.
- **What the site must show him:** every demo answer cites its source filing/circular/KB doc.

### P3. Support / Operations Head — "Meera"
- **Goal:** Reduce repetitive tickets and phone load; smooth human handoff.
- **Needs:** Deflection metrics, escalation-to-human, multilingual coverage, easy KB updates.
- **Frustrations:** Ticket-only flows (à la Zerodha); limited support hours; language gaps.
- **Decision criteria:** Handoff quality, coverage hours, admin simplicity.
- **What the site must show her:** clean escalation flow and 24/7 multilingual coverage.

## SECONDARY — End-investors (widget users, shown in demos)

### S1. Tier-2/3 Retail Investor (Hindi/Tamil-first) — "Karthik / Suresh"
- **Goal:** Understand a stock, a filing, or how to act — in his own language.
- **Needs:** Hindi/Tamil answers, plain explanations, trustworthy sources, mobile-first.
- **Frustrations:** English-only finance jargon; no Tamil support anywhere today.

### S2. NALCO Retail Shareholder — "Lakshmi"
- **Goal:** Make sense of NALCO disclosures/results without reading raw filings.
- **Needs:** Plain-language filing explanation + citation to the actual disclosure.

### S3. Algo-curious Trader — "Vikram"
- **Goal:** Learn SEBI/NSE algo rules, white-box vs black-box, empanelled vendors — safely.
- **Needs:** Accurate, regulation-cited education (seed data already provided in workspace).

## Persona → design implication
| Persona | Must prove on the site |
|---|---|
| Ananya (Product) | Embeddability + multilingual reach + demo |
| Rahul (Compliance) | Citations + disclaimers + governance |
| Meera (Support) | Escalation + coverage + admin ease |
| End-investors | EN/HI/TA answers with citations, mobile-first |
